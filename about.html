About Us

Chellie Online Shop is an online business founded on August 6, 2017 on Facebook and now on Lazada. Chellie's Online Shop is located in Calamba, Laguna. We sell products like clothes, cosmetics, learning materials for kids, footwear, bags, and health essentials like medicine, vitamins, and necessities like facemasks and motorcycle accessories. You can check our Facebook page by searching for @chellieonlineshop. You can also contact us via Facebook and Lazada.
